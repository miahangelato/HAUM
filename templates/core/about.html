ABOUT US - HAU MARKETPLACE

At HAUM (Holy Angel University Marketplace), we are driven by the evolving landscape of digital commerce and the transformative power of online marketplaces in response to the COVID-19 pandemic. As the world swiftly transitioned to the digital realm, we recognized the need to create a platform tailored to the unique needs of our fellow students at Holy Angel University. Our journey began with an understanding of the changing dynamics in the marketplace, where the demand for secondhand goods and sustainability was on the rise. We saw students, seeking innovative solutions to fund their education and the challenges they faced due to financial constraints. In response, HAUM was born as a student-centric online marketplace, designed to be user-friendly and efficient, connecting students within our campus community.


Our mission is deeply rooted in sustainability, affordability, and community engagement. We understand the annual cycle of students graduating or progressing to higher academic years, leaving behind a wealth of products, from textbooks to electronics. These items, which students no longer need, can be valuable assets to others. Our platform offers a convenient space for sellers to find new homes for their pre-loved items, with the primary goal of generating extra income. Simultaneously, buyers benefit from access to a wide range of products at budget-friendly prices. HAUM embraces the environmental consciousness of our times, recognizing the increasing demand for secondhand, sustainable goods.


As students ourselves at Holy Angel University, we have a profound understanding of the challenges and opportunities that lie within our campus community. HAUM aims to streamline the buying and selling process by providing an accessible, centralized platform for students. We leverage the advantages of online marketplaces, including ease of use, affordability, and a good shopping experience. Our commitment to fostering a strong sense of community within our university is at the heart of HAUM. In summary, HAUM is not just a marketplace; it's a substantial and easily understood program that benefits our entire university community, aligning with the changing dynamics of the marketplace and the evolving needs of our student body.

Objectives

  1. Create a Campus Marketplace:
    The primary objective is to establish a digital marketplace called "HAUM" within the Holy Angel University campus, aimed at facilitating the buying and selling of both used and new goods.
  2. Support Students' Financial Needs:
    This website seeks to provide students with an opportunity to supplement their income by selling items they no longer need, thereby addressing financial challenges that some students face.
  3. Promote Sustainability:
    By encouraging the reuse of items through secondhand sales, this website aims to promote sustainability and reduce waste, aligning with the increasing consumer demand for environmentally friendly and sustainable products.
  4. Enhance User-Friendliness:
    This website's goal is to create a user-friendly and efficient online platform for students to engage in buying and selling activities, improving the overall experience for users.
  5. Boost Convenience:
    By bringing together buyers and sellers within the university community through an online marketplace, this website intends to enhance convenience and accessibility for students in acquiring the products they need.
  6. Foster Community Engagement:
    This website aspires to strengthen the sense of community within the university by providing a platform for students to interact and transact with each other.
  7. Offer Cost Savings:
    For buyers, this website seeks to offer cost savings by allowing them to purchase products at lower prices compared to new equivalents, promoting affordability.
  8. Support a Range of Products:
    The website's marketplace will cater to a wide variety of products, from textbooks and school supplies to electronics and more, meeting the diverse needs of students.
  9. Maximize Market Potential:
    By tapping into the market for secondhand goods and digital commerce, the website aims to harness the potential for growth and participation in online retail, particularly among students.
